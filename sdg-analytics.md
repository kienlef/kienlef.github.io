# SDG analytics is useful only when context, uncertainty, and responsibility stay visible.

<p class="kf-page-lead">SDG Analytics uses public Sustainable Development Goal data to teach responsible data literacy, visualization, and multi-indicator interpretation.</p>

<div class="kf-grid-2">
  <div class="kf-card"><div class="kf-icon"><i class="ph ph-compass"></i></div><h3>Orientation</h3><p>Understand the goal, indicator, country, year, and data source before interpreting results.</p></div>
  <div class="kf-card"><div class="kf-icon"><i class="ph ph-database"></i></div><h3>Data preparation</h3><p>Load, clean, document, and inspect public data before modeling or visualization.</p></div>
  <div class="kf-card"><div class="kf-icon"><i class="ph ph-chart-line-up"></i></div><h3>Visualization</h3><p>Make patterns readable without hiding gaps, uncertainty, or limitations.</p></div>
  <div class="kf-card"><div class="kf-icon"><i class="ph ph-scales"></i></div><h3>Responsible interpretation</h3><p>Compare indicators carefully and avoid unsupported causal claims.</p></div>
</div>
